Birth Anniversary of Bhagwan Birsa Munda JANJATIYA GAURAV DIWAS


St. Xavier’s College, Ranchi organized Janjatiya Gaurav Diwas at the occasion of Birth Day Anniversary of Bhagwan Birsa Munda on 15th November, 2022. Shri Mahadeo Toppo was the Chief Guest for the occasion. In his key note address he highlighted the significance of ‘ULGULAAN’ of Birsa Munda. Dr. Fr. Nabor Lakra SJ the Principal and Dr. Fr. Alexius Ekka SJ the Rector of the College were the Guests of honor. Various competitions like Drawing Competition, Speech Competition and Essay Writing Competition were organized under the care of the Committee for the students. About 182 registered online for the various competitions.

ABOUT US

St. Xavier’s College, Ranchi is a Minority Educational Institution based on religion established and administered by the Ranchi Jesuit Province of the Society of Jesus. The College is registered by the Ranchi Province under the local title ‘Xaviers Ranchi’ under the Society’s Registration Act XXI of 1860 and affiliated to Ranchi University Ranchi.
